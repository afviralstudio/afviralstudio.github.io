Orb Trap: Merge Puzzle

Orb Trap is a neon merge puzzle game inspired by classic 2048-style and 2248-style gameplay. Merge orbs, create long chains, unlock higher tiers and chase your best score across multiple game modes.

Features include Slide Fusion, Orb Drop, Place Orb, Chain and Link modes, daily rewards, special actions and online leaderboards.
